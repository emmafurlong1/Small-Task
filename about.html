About Us

Our centre is based in Ormskirk and is home to many species of Owls, Hawks, Falcons, our Eagles Freedom and Patriot, along with our family of Ferrets and our family of 3 Red Pandas. We have an on site Gift Shop, Education Centre, Medical Room & Rehabilitation Area, Outdoor Flying Area, Indoor Training Area, Large Weathering Lawn for Falcons and Hawks, alongside our many Large Aviaries. Hot & Cold Drinks are available on site.

The centre is open Weds - Sunday 9am - 2pm for admissions.
Adults - £7.50
Children 3 and over - £4.50 (Under 3s Free)
Senior Citizens - £4.50
